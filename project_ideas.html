Project Ideas

Genetic Architecture of Adaptation

  • Uses existing GWAS and demographic model.
  • Build CNN to predict parameters of an optimum shift model Stetter 2018

Estimate migration surface for maize Mexico

  • Uses SEEDs data of ~2500 landraces in Mexico
  • Run Petkova 2016 or similar methods

Recent bottlenecks due to European contact

  • Uses landrace data from C2 (unpublished) or other samples
  • IBD or other approaches to identify recent bottleneck

Geographic distribution of incompatibility loci

  • Uses public data on teosintes from Swarts et al and SEEDs
  • Align to published cloned sequences of tcb1 and ga1 female and ga1 male

Dating everything

  • Uses Albers to build model to date SNPs
  • Estimate age of lots of cool SNPs – tga1, etc.

Driving miss genome

  • Uses new assembly of Ab10 to determine reads that can be used to genotype kindr and trkin.
  • Do GWAS on flowering time, other traits, association with elevation, population frequencies, etc. of drive loci.
