R-I Lab members

PI



Jeffrey Ross-Ibarra

Jeff is Professor and Section Chair in the Department of Plant Sciences. He started at UC Davis in 2009.




Lab Manager

Anne Lorant

Anne has worked as a lab manager since November of 2012, and is in charge of overseeing lab and greenhouse experiments.




Postdoctoral Scholars

Luis Avila

Luis joined the lab in March 2016. He is working on recombination rate variation in maize landraces and teosinte.






Josh Hough

Josh joined the lab in February 2016 to work on linked selection and the evolution of recombination in maize and teosinte.




Emily Josephs

Emily came to UC Davis in January 2016. She is an NSF plant genome postdoc working on adaptation to domestication in collaboration with Graham Coop and Julin Maloof.




Jinliang Yang

Jinliang joined the lab in October 2014 to work on maize quantitative genetics and the genetics of nitrogen fixation. His PhD work included new GWAS methods, the genetics of heterosis, and algorithm development for working with SNP data.

Graduate Students

Paul Bilinski

Paul is a 5th year student in Plant Biology, working on the evolution of genome size and repeat sequences in Zea and Tripsacum.




Anna O’Brien

Anna is a 4th year student in Population Biology, coadvised by Sharon Strauss. Anna works on the coevolution of teosinte and mycorrhiza.




Michelle Stitzer

Michelle is a 2nd year student in Population Biology, working on the population genetics of transposable elements in maize and teosinte as part of the maize diversity project.




Dianne Velasco

Dianne is a 5th year student in Integrated Genetics and Genomics, working on the genomics of Prunus.
