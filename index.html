Ross-Ibarra Lab

Welcome to the Ross-Ibarra Lab in the Dept. of Plant Sciences at the University of California Davis. Our lab works on the evolutionary genetics of maize and teosinte. Click through images and links to see who we are and what we do, and check out the news and lab docs for updates and latest happenings.
